Russia

Russia has clear reasons to try to keep Assad in power, in order to benefit their military. Russia's only naval base in the Mediterranean region is at the Syrian port of Tartus (Ted Galen Carpenter), which would most likely cease to exist if the Assad regime fell.

China

China's motivations to participate are much less strong, as it is purely economic. "China was Syria's largest trading partner in 2011, with Syrian exports to that country totaling more than $2.4 billion. China is also a major participant in Syria's oil industry" (Ted Galen Carpenter)

United States

The United States itself has no reason to participate in Syria, other than to supposedly "save the people". However, it's allies have their own reasons to want to remove Assad, and the US wants to keep Russia from gaining power.
